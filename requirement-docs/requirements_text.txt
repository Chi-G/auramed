enabling efficient management of patient records, appointments, treatments, 
prescriptions, and billing. The system will serve healthcare practitioners, nurses, and 
administrative staff with role-based access to ensure secure handling of medical data.

5. PROBLEM STATEMENT 
The growing need for efficient healthcare delivery requires a cohesive digital system 
where clinics can manage patient information, track appointments, document clinical 
encounters, manage pharmacy inventory, and handle billing in one platform. Existing 
solutions are often fragmented, expensive, or poorly suited for small -to-medium 
clinics. There is a significant need for an affordable, integrated system that streamlines 
clinical workflows while maintaining data security and audit compliance. 
 
6. PROPOSED SOLUTION 
The Clinical Management System will integrate patient records, appointments, clinical 
visits, treatments, prescriptions, pharmacy management, and billing into one unified 
web-based platform. The system will ensure accessibility across desktop and tablet 
devices, provide role -based access for different staff levels, and maintain 
comprehensive audit logs for compliance. It will feature automated billing calculations, 
stock alerts, and detailed reporting to support clinic decision-making.

This agreement shall commence on the date of signing and continue until project 
completion and warranty period expiration (approximately 3 months). 
Termination Clause: 
• Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days' written notice 
• Client remains responsible for payment for all work completed up to 
termination date 
• Upon termination, Forahia will deliver all completed work and documentation 
prorated to payment received

will be delivered

IP remains with Forahia until payment is settled 
Suspension: 
• Project may be suspended for up to 30 days at Client's request (timeline adjusts 
accordingly)
